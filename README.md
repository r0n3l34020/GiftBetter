# GiftBetter
GiftBetter helps maintain friendships! Have you ever had someones birthday coming up and dont know what to get for them? Maybe you forgot it was even their birthday! GiftBetter not only reminds you in advance, but provides personalized gift ideas based on the receiver and your OWN persona!

## What is GiftBetter?

Giftbetter is a web-app that stores information about you and your significant other, helping you remember they birthdays, gift request, gift recommendations and previous gift-list all-in-one! The perfect birthday or anniversary or graduation day buddy, GiftBetter stores your information through Supabase, and receives its AI recommendations through a built-in Gemini API. 

## Why did I create GiftBetter?

I created GiftBetter because this year, I somehow forgot my mom's birthday! It was really embarassing, and when I came home from school to wish her after realizing, her friends were already there, handing her their own gifts! I was empty-handed, which disappointed everyone in the room. That's why I created GiftBetter, so you don't freeze up when someone's birthday is tomorrow and don't know what to get them!
